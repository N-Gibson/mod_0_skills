Class: Dish

Attributes: 

			Number of ingredient (integer)

			Type of ingredient (string)

			Complete (boolean)

			Input to computer (boolean)


Methods: 

			How many ingredients are in this dish? (the method calculates how many ingredients are in a specific dish, modifies attribute: number of ingredients)

			What ingredients are in this dish? (modifies the kind of ingredients required to make a specific dish, modifies attribute: type of ingredient)

			Has the dish been made? (calculates whether or not a dish is completed, modifies attribute: complete)

			Hasthe dish has been ordered/ finished? (assesses whether or not the dish has been entered into the computer either before or after its been made, modifies attribute: input to computer)
